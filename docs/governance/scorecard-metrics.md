## Evidence discipline

- If a metric does not have a trustworthy measurement yet, report the evidence gap explicitly instead of backfilling with opinion.
- Re-audit checkpoints should refresh `overall health score`, `hotspot count`, and `direct-foreign-read count`.
- Operational reviews should refresh `TTD`, `TTR`, and `queue-failure count` once the underlying evidence exists.
