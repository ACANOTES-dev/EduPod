# EduPod Product Brochure

## The All-in-One School Operating System

EduPod is not a narrow point solution. It is a full school management information system designed to run the day-to-day life of a school from admissions to enrolment, from teaching and learning to billing and payroll, from parent communications to safeguarding, compliance, and leadership reporting.

Built for schools that want one connected platform instead of a patchwork of disconnected tools, EduPod brings academic management, school operations, family engagement, finance, wellbeing, compliance, and public-facing school workflows into one system.

For buyers, the core value is simple: fewer systems, less duplication, stronger visibility, better control, and a much better experience for staff, families, and leadership.

---

## What EduPod Is

EduPod is a multi-tenant school operating platform that supports:

- student information and family records
- admissions and onboarding
- academic structure and class management
- attendance, gradebook, report cards, and transcripts
- homework and school diary workflows
- timetabling, substitutions, and room planning
- finance, fees, invoicing, payments, and payroll
- announcements, parent messaging, and engagement workflows
- behaviour, safeguarding, pastoral care, and early warning
- SEN support planning and resource allocation
- GDPR, legal, and regulatory operations
- school websites and public admissions journeys
- leadership dashboards, analytics, and board reporting

This is a system for the whole school, not just the office, not just teachers, and not just parents.

### One platform instead of many

EduPod replaces the usual sprawl of separate tools for MIS, grading, timetabling, communications, fee management, parent forms, safeguarding tracking, reporting, and compliance.

The platform goes beyond basic record-keeping. - October Returns readiness workflows
- DES returns and mappings
- anti-bullying reporting support

### Strong parent, teacher, and leadership experiences

EduPod is not only an admin back office. It also includes:

- parent-facing dashboards and engagement flows
- teacher-facing attendance, grading, timetable, and homework tools
- leadership reporting, compliance dashboards, workload visibility, and operational oversight

### Bilingual and inclusive

EduPod supports English and Arabic with full RTL support, making it suitable for schools serving diverse communities.
