## Execution Phases

The plan is organised into 5 execution phases. Each phase's items are ordered by priority within the phase.

- **Phase A**: Critical operational safety (do first, nothing else matters if production breaks)
- **Phase B**: Security hardening (close remaining gaps in the strongest dimension)
- **Phase C**: Test foundation (build the safety net before touching architecture)
- **Phase D**: Architecture and modularity (refactor with confidence)
- **Phase E**: Polish and maturity (the last mile to 9.5)
