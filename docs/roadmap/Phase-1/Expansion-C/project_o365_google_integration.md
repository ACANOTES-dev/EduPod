**Priority:** Should-have. Significant workflow convenience. One of VSware's genuine strengths.

**What it is:**
Automatic provisioning and management of Microsoft 365 or Google Workspace accounts for students and staff, synced from EduPod's data.

**What EduPod should support:**

- **Account provisioning:** When a student/staff member is created in EduPod, automatically create their O365/Google account with standardised username format
- **Class list sync:** Teaching groups in EduPod automatically reflected as Teams/Classroom groups
- **Group email addresses:** Auto-generated class/year-group distribution lists
- **SSO:** Single sign-on so staff log into EduPod with their school Microsoft/Google credentials
- **OneDrive/Google Drive integration:** Link student portfolios or document storage
- **Deprovisioning:** When a student leaves, automatically disable/archive their account

**Competitor status:**

- VSware: native O365 integration — automatic account provisioning, class list sync, group emails, SSO. Consistently praised in testimonials.
- Compass: integration via SalamanderSoft partner (indirect, not native)

**Why it matters:**

- Irish schools are overwhelmingly Microsoft 365 (government licensing deals make O365 near-universal)
- Without this, IT coordinators manually manage hundreds of accounts — a major time sink
- SSO eliminates password fatigue for teachers
- Class list sync means teachers don't maintain separate rosters in Teams

**How to apply:** Build after statutory integrations and behaviour management. This is a strong demo feature ("watch it auto-create 200 student accounts in 30 seconds") and a genuine admin time-saver. Start with Microsoft 365 (dominant in Irish schools), add Google Workspace later.
