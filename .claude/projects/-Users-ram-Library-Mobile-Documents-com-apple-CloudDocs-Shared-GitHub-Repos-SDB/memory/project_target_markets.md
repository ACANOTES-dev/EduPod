EduPod is an Irish product. Ireland is the target market — no other markets are being pursued.

**Why:** The product is built to serve Irish schools (primary and post-primary). All compliance, statutory reporting (Tusla, DES Returns, P-POD), payroll (Irish PAYE/PRSI/USC), and integration decisions (HEAnet/SMIS framework) are Ireland-specific.

**How to apply:** When making architecture or feature decisions, optimise for the Irish education system. Do not design for hypothetical international markets. The multilingual support exists because Irish schools have diverse student populations, not because the product targets other countries.
