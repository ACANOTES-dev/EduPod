Option A vs Option B — What's different?

Global Navigation A B

A: Hub bar pinned to the top — text pills, always visible, clean and traditional.
B: Floating dock at the bottom — icon-based, hoverable, app-like, more spatial. Frees up the entire top of the page.

Home Layout A B

A: Uniform grid of equal-sized zone cards. Clean, predictable, easy to scan.
B: Bento grid with varied card sizes. Priority actions get a hero card. Feels more editorial, more Apple, more dynamic.

Module Depth A B

A: Contextual sidebar appears inside complex modules. Grouped sub-pages. Familiar depth pattern.
B: Horizontal tabs for everything — no sidebar anywhere. Full-width content always. Simpler but may get crowded for 12+ sub-pages.

Overall Vibe A B

A: Clean web app. Linear meets ClassDojo. Familiar but elevated. Immediate comprehension.
B: Native app feel. Apple meets Arc. More bold, more spatial. Feels like a platform, not a website.
