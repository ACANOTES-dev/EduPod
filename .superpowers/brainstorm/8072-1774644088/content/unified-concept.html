How the three layers work together

SHELL

C's Hub Bar — The Global Navigation

Always visible. 7 pills: Home, People, Learning, Operations, Finance, Reports, Settings. This is the constant — it never changes, never overwhelms. Works identically on desktop and mobile (collapses to a bottom tab bar). Scales to any number of features because the domains are stable.

HOME

A's Spatial Workspace — The Dashboard

When "Home" is active, you see the living workspace with zone cards showing live data from every domain. This is where the magic lives — the platform showing you what matters before you ask. Each zone card is a portal into its domain. Clicking one is identical to clicking the hub pill.

DEPTH

B's Module Sidebar — The Deep Navigation

Inside complex modules (Finance, Scheduling, Behaviour, Payroll), a contextual sidebar appears with that module's sub-pages grouped logically. Simple modules (Attendance, Gradebook) don't need it — they go straight to content. The sidebar only appears when the module is deep enough to warrant it.

The result: Zero sidebar clutter on first impression. Spatial, alive dashboard. Full-width content on every page. Deep modules get the navigation depth they need. Simple pages stay simple. And it all lives under the same clean hub bar that works perfectly on mobile, tablet, and desktop.
